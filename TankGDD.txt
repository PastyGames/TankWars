Concept
A 1v1 Tank Shooting game, Player vs. Ai. Objective of the game is to hit
opponent enough to cause their tank to destroy(die). Once one tank is
destroyed the game will end.
Possibly create more enemies on level progression!

Rules
Player and Ai have a set amount of health pool
Player and AI cant drive out of the map
Shooting of tank should decrease health
Game should end once one loses all health

Sound
A sound should play when the shooting happens

Effects
Gun explosing effect on opposite tank
smoke coming from the shot
